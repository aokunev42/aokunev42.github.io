I am now a math postdoc at Penn State university, before that I was a postdoc at Loughborogh University (UK). I got my PhD at Higher School of Economics, where my advisor was Yulij Ilyashenko. For more information see my Curriculum Vitae.

Research

I work in dynamical systems, with recent research focused on differential equations and their physical applications, including the averaging method. My PhD research was in discrete dynamics, where I worked on problems involving iterated function systems, attractors, hyperbolicity, and partial hyperbolicity. For more information see my research statement and publications listed below.
